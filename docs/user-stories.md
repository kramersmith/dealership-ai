# User Stories

## Customer (Buyer)

### Onboarding
- As a buyer who's preparing ahead of time, I want a full research flow (enter vehicle, analyze history, build game plan, set target prices) so I arrive at the dealership fully prepared.
- As a buyer who just arrived at the dealership, I want a fast-start flow (enter vehicle and price, get instant assessment and quick checklist) so I get help immediately without lengthy setup.

### Voice mode
- As a buyer driving to the dealership, I want to talk to the AI instead of typing so I can get advice hands-free.
- As a buyer walking the lot, I want to speak to the AI discreetly so I don't have to pull out my phone and type.

### Pre-visit
- As a buyer, I want to enter vehicle details (VIN, or year/make/model/trim/mileage/condition) and get a plain-English risk assessment so I know what I'm walking into before I drive to the dealership.
- As a buyer, I want to compare two or more vehicles side-by-side so I can decide which one is worth the trip.
- As a buyer, I want to upload a CARFAX report and have the AI flag red flags so I don't have to know what to look for myself.
- As a buyer, I want to enter a vehicle and price and see how the deal compares to actual recent transaction prices in my market so I know if the asking price is fair.
- As a buyer, I want to input my credit score, budget, and vehicle details and get a complete game plan — target price, walk-away price, what to inspect, and what to say — so I feel prepared.
- As a buyer, I want to know what financing rate I should expect for my credit profile and this specific vehicle so I can spot a markup.
- As a buyer, I want a checklist of what to bring to the dealership so nothing slows me down or gives them leverage.

### At the dealership
- As a buyer, I want to record the conversation from my phone so the AI can analyze what was said in real time or after the fact.
- As a buyer, I want the AI to flag when something said verbally contradicts the paperwork so I catch discrepancies before signing.
- As a buyer, I want a record of verbal price agreements, F&I disclosures, and add-on pitches so I have proof of what was promised.
- As a buyer, I want the AI to detect pressure tactics in the conversation so I'm aware when I'm being manipulated.
- As a buyer, I want to snap a photo of the deal sheet and instantly see a breakdown of every line item in plain English so I understand what I'm being charged.
- As a buyer, I want to text the AI what the salesperson just said and get a suggested response so I don't freeze or fold under pressure.
- As a buyer, I want the AI to tell me "walk" or "stay" based on the current deal parameters so I have a rational voice countering my emotions.
- As a buyer, I want to input the dealer's offered APR and see whether it's a markup so I don't overpay on financing.
- As a buyer, I want to know which F&I add-ons are worth considering for my specific situation (e.g. GAP with low down payment) and which to decline.
- As a buyer, I want a "what am I forgetting?" prompt that asks me the right questions before I sign so I don't miss anything.
- As a buyer, I want to upload a photo of the final contract and have the AI cross-check it against what was verbally agreed so I catch last-minute changes.

### Persistent UI (alongside chat)
- As a buyer, I want to see what phase of the deal I'm in (Researching → At Dealership → Negotiating → F&I → Signing → Post-Purchase) so I always know where I am in the process.
- As a buyer, I want to see my target price, walk-away price, and the dealer's current offer side-by-side so I can instantly judge any number they throw at me.
- As a buyer, I want a running OTD calculation that updates as numbers come in so I always know the real total cost.
- As a buyer, I want to see my expected APR vs. the dealer's offered APR so I can spot a markup at a glance.
- As a buyer, I want a phase-appropriate checklist that updates automatically so I don't have to remember what to do next.
- As a buyer, I want a vehicle card showing the car I'm looking at (specs, price, risk flags, market comparison) so the key facts are always visible.
- As a buyer, I want a negotiation scorecard (their starting price → current offer → my target) so I can see progress visually.
- As a buyer, I want red/yellow/green indicators on price, rate, and terms so I can assess the deal at a glance.
- As a buyer, I want quick action buttons ("Analyze this photo", "What do I say?", "Should I walk?") so I can get help with one tap.
- As a buyer, I want to see how long I've been at the dealership so I stay aware of time-based pressure tactics.
- As a buyer, I want a reminder if I've been waiting a long time so I know it might be a deliberate tactic.

### Notifications and follow-up
- As a buyer who walked away, I want a push notification when the dealer calls or texts with context on where the deal left off and a suggested response so I'm prepared in the moment.
- As a buyer, I want post-deal notifications for refinance timing, recall alerts, and maintenance milestones so I stay on top of my purchase.

### Session / deal management
- As a buyer, I want to start a new chat for a new vehicle or dealer so each deal has its own context.
- As a buyer, I want to add previous chats as context to a new chat so the AI knows my history (e.g. "I already analyzed this vehicle" or "here's what happened at the first dealer").
- As a buyer shopping multiple dealers, I want to have multiple active deals so I can compare and switch between them.
- As a buyer, I want my deal history to persist so I can pick up where I left off if a dealer contacts me days later.

### Buyer power features
- As a buyer, I want to broadcast my current offer to other dealers in the area so they can compete for my business.
- As a buyer, I want to see total cost of ownership (fuel, insurance, maintenance, depreciation) so I understand the real cost beyond the purchase price.
- As a buyer, I want to get insurance quotes inside the app before signing so I'm not surprised by the cost after.
- As a buyer, I want to compare third-party warranty options at real prices instead of just declining the dealer's warranty so I can make an informed decision.
- As a buyer, I want to book a pre-purchase inspection with a nearby mechanic directly from the app so I don't skip this step out of laziness.
- As a buyer, I want the app to check partner credit unions and lenders for better rates after the dealer makes an offer so I get the best financing automatically.

### Post-purchase
- As a buyer, I want a post-purchase checklist (verify contract, plan refinance, immediate maintenance) so I protect my investment.
- As a buyer, I want to know when and how to refinance based on my credit profile so I can lower my rate after the deal.
- As a buyer, I want to see my deal score compared to other buyers of the same vehicle in my market so I know how I did.
- As a buyer, I want to see my lifetime savings from using the app so I can see the value and share it with others.

## Salesperson

### Before the customer arrives
- As a salesperson, I want to see AI-suggested talking points based on the vehicle I'm about to show so I'm prepared for common objections.
- As a salesperson, I want to quickly generate multiple deal structures (lease vs. finance, different terms and down payments) that hit a buyer's stated budget so I can desk a deal faster.

### On the floor
- As a salesperson, I want real-time suggested responses when a buyer raises an objection so I always have a strong answer.
- As a salesperson, I want the AI to capture buyer preferences and signals from the conversation automatically so I don't have to take notes or remember everything for CRM entry.
- As a salesperson, I want to press a button to alert my manager with a summary of the conversation so they arrive informed and ready for the T.O.
- As a salesperson, I want AI-generated competitive comparisons when a buyer mentions another vehicle or dealer so I can respond with facts instead of guessing.
- As a salesperson, I want the AI to surface incentives and rebates the buyer qualifies for that I might not know about so I can build trust and lower the effective price.
- As a salesperson, I want communication style insights about the buyer so I can adapt my approach (analytical vs. emotional, price-driven vs. feature-driven).

### Post-interaction
- As a salesperson, I want the AI to auto-populate my CRM with structured data from the conversation so I don't have to manually enter notes.
- As a salesperson, I want a deal replay that shows where I lost a deal so I can improve without needing a manager to sit in.
- As a salesperson, I want AI-generated follow-up messages personalized based on the actual conversation so my follow-ups are relevant, not generic.

### Training
- As a salesperson, I want to practice against AI-generated customer scenarios so I can improve my skills without needing a manager or ride-along.
- As a salesperson, I want the AI to simulate specific objections (interest rate, price, mileage concerns) so I can rehearse my responses.
- As a new salesperson, I want to run through simulated deals end-to-end so I can build confidence before working live customers.

## Sales/F&I Manager

### During active deals
- As a manager, I want a real-time summary of each active deal on the floor so I know what's happening without hovering.
- As a manager, I want to be notified when a salesperson needs me, with conversation context attached, so I arrive prepared.
- As a manager, I want AI-suggested deal structures when desking so I can quickly find a scenario that works for both sides.
- As a manager, I want to know which customers on the lot are most likely to buy today so I can prioritize my time.

### Post-deal
- As a manager, I want a predicted CSI score for each deal so I can intervene on at-risk customers before the survey goes out.
- As a manager, I want to review AI-analyzed deal replays for my team so I can coach more efficiently.
- As a manager, I want a follow-up plan auto-generated for each customer so no one falls through the cracks.
- As a manager, I want to see anonymized competitive intelligence (who buyers compare us to, top reasons for walking) so I can adjust our approach.
- As a manager, I want AI-powered BDC responses for internet leads so initial responses are personalized and fast, not generic templates.
- As a manager, I want to assign AI training simulations to my team and track their progress so I can develop skills at scale.

## Compliance Officer

### Ongoing
- As a compliance officer, I want real-time alerts when an F&I conversation includes charges the customer hasn't consented to so I can intervene before it becomes a liability.
- As a compliance officer, I want identity verification flags when buyer statements don't match their documents so I comply with the FTC Red Flags Rule.

## Analytics & Learning

### Buyer-side
- As a buyer, I want to report whether the dealer called me back after I walked away so the AI learns when walking is effective.
- As a buyer, I want to log my final deal outcome (price, rate, terms) so the AI can compare its advice to real results and improve.
- As a buyer, I want the AI's advice to be informed by what actually worked for other buyers at this dealership so I get smarter recommendations.
- As a buyer, I want to see data like "X% of buyers who walked away from this dealer got a callback" so I can make informed decisions.
- As a buyer, I want to rate which negotiation scripts worked and which didn't so the AI improves its suggestions over time.

### Salesperson-side
- As a salesperson, I want to see which of my objection responses led to closes vs. lost deals so I know what's working.
- As a salesperson, I want to see my average time-to-close and where my deals tend to stall so I can improve.
- As a salesperson, I want to know which communication approaches work best for different buyer types so I can adapt faster.
- As a salesperson, I want to see my own performance trends over time so I can track my improvement.

### Manager-side
- As a manager, I want to see where in the process deals fall apart most often so I can address systemic issues.
- As a manager, I want to know T.O. effectiveness — does my involvement improve close rates, and when is the optimal time to step in?
- As a manager, I want per-salesperson performance breakdowns by deal phase so I can coach targeted skills, not generic training.
- As a manager, I want to see which F&I products get accepted vs. declined and at what price points so I can optimize the menu.
- As a manager, I want lost deal analysis — common reasons, patterns by vehicle type or price range — so I can recover more deals.
- As a manager, I want to see customer sentiment trends (arriving more informed, more resistant?) so I can adapt our approach.

## Transaction Types

### New vs. used cars
- As a buyer of a new car, I want the AI to focus on MSRP/invoice pricing, manufacturer incentives, and F&I rather than vehicle condition so the advice fits my situation.
- As a buyer of a used car, I want the AI to focus on vehicle history, condition assessment, and fair market value so I understand the risk I'm taking.
- As a buyer, I want the app to adapt its checklists, benchmarks, and advice based on whether I'm buying new or used so nothing is irrelevant.

### Leases
- As a buyer considering a lease, I want the AI to break down money factor, residual value, MSD, acquisition fee, and disposition fee in plain English so I actually understand the lease structure.
- As a buyer, I want to compare lease vs. finance scenarios side-by-side so I can make an informed decision.
- As a buyer, I want the AI to flag lease terms that are unfavorable (high money factor, low residual, excessive fees) so I don't get locked into a bad deal.

### Private party sales
- As a buyer in a private party sale, I want to use the vehicle analyzer, CARFAX tool, and "is this a good deal?" benchmark so I have the same protection as at a dealership.
- As a buyer in a private party sale, I want a checklist of what to verify (title status, lien check, bill of sale) so I don't miss critical steps.
